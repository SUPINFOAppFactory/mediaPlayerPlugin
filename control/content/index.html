The audio player allows your users to do some amazing things, such as:

  • - Control the audio in your app
  • - Add tracks to a master playlist from a variety of plugins like the Media Center
  • - Manage their audio settings like looping a track and remembering the last position

This functionality is even built in for you, so there is nothing for you to manage.

We definitely recommend adding this to your side menu so that your users have quick access to it. To manage your side menu, click on "Plugins" in the left hand menu.
